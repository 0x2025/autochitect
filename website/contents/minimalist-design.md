# Why Minimalist Design Wins

Modern web design is often cluttered with giant hero images, scroll animations, and unnecessary whitespace. For a technical blog, these are distractions.

A minimalist design, inspired by the classics (like lwn.net or drudge), prioritizes:

- **Text over Image**: Focus on the content.
- **Speed**: Minimal CSS and JS for instant loading.
- **Information Density**: Allow users to see more headlines and summaries at once.

Our new "Autonomous Architect" theme follows these principles to ensure that the focus remains on code and architecture.
